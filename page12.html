Problem Solving:

 Account Issues

I'm Sorry To hear that you are having some account issues. Lets try to figure this out:

1.    Make sure you followed all of the steps and didn't leave anything blank.

2.    If you have just recently created an account, it will most likley be ready in a few hours. If not, then please Email or Call us.  

3.    Make sure you are logged into the email that you used to create the account.

4.    Make sure you have not created more than One account within a few days.

5.    Maybe your internet is slow?

6.    If you forgot your password, please Email or Call us.

7.    Make sure you used OUR account creator. If Not, Click Here 

8.    If you accidantly logged into someone elses account, Please do not copy or delete their information. That is against our Terms Of Service!                                                        

                         8A.  Please log out of that account and contact us via Email.

9.    If you are having issues downloading you photos from you account, try logging out, then log back in.

10.    If NONE of this has helped fix your problem please continue to Call us.


                                                                                                   

 Basic Questions

1.     How Many Days Before My Event Should I Hire A Photographer?

        Answer: We reccomend to Hire a photographer 5 days prior to your event. This will give the photographer time to prepare for the event.

2.    How Do I Get My Photos?

        Answer: Simply Log into your account or Create An Account. You MUST have an account with us to download your photos!


3.    Refunds?

        Answer: Sorry, we do not give refunds. If you are not satisfied with our service, you can have a photographer retake the photos. 

4.    It Wont Allow Me To Download My Photos!

        Answer: We are sorry to hear that, Please attempt to refresh the page. If it still doesn't work, Please Email or Call us.
